Why this page exists

Quoting looks trivial until you repeat it across 30 or 300 values. This page keeps it consistent and reversible so you do not bounce between editor macros, spreadsheet formulas, and shell snippets.

FAQ

Can I switch between single quotes and double quotes?

Yes. Use the presets for common output or change the quote character directly for custom wrapping.

Will it remove quotes from comma-separated input?

Yes. Pick the matching CSV preset, switch to unquote mode, and the tool will split on the configured delimiter before stripping the quote character.

Can I output one quoted item per line instead of CSV?

Yes. Use the line-based presets or set the delimiter field to \n for a vertical output list.
